Neumann in Central Plaza

Kill Brahm (Duel quest) to access this quest.

NEED

ITEM

QT.

Light Crystal Fragment

 3

Order:

  1. Return to your nation's capital
  2. Catch a trigger spot near the entrance to get a letter
  3. Click on the pop-ups to read the letter
  4. Talk to Neumann, Central Plaza
  5. Take 3 Light Crytal Fragments if you can, you'll need them
  6. Exit Astir
  7. Go to Northern Soplar Highway
  8. Talk to Confederation Soldier (L-7)
  9. Go to the Foothills of Mt. Jorito
  10. Visit G-6 to trigger a cutscene
  11. Get 3 Light Crystal Fragments from Kobolds
  12. Bring them to G-6
  13. Get close to the cave entrance to trigger the cutscene

Light Crystal Fragments drop in Mt. Jorito from:

  • Kobolds Lv24
  • Kobold Raiders Lv32
  • Kobold Soliders Lv38

Reward:

  • Access to El Behd

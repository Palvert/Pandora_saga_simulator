Nelhaunt on Confederation Road (L-11)

Need:

  • 32 Level
  • Kill Boa Lv33 - x10
  • or (Big slugs) Lv31 - x10
  • or Cockatrices Lv30 - x10

Order:

  1. Talk to Nelhaunt
  2. Note location and monster and attack type
  3. Go south and take the road going west
  4. Go to Shahti (G-7).
  5. > Give location ( In the Foothills of Mt. Jorito )
  6. > Give monster ( the Cockatrices )
  7. > Give attack type ( caught us in a pincher attack )
  8. Kill 10 of the monsters at the location
  9. Talk to Shahti

Additional inforamtion:

  • All of them can be found in the Foothills of Mt. Jorito

Reward:

  • 10000 gold
  • 250000 xp
